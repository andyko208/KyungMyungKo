Research Interests

  • Multimodal learning: Vision and audio are the primary sources of data that we continuosly perceive from the world. Our brain leverages these cues to reason about them. As such, our world could be explained as us humans experiencing immersive video. My research attempts to understand how these modalities are incorporated to create individual world within us. Ultimately, my goal is to leverage them to construct a synthetic world. I am specifically interested in video understanding and video generation.
